About Law Talk

Law Talk is a business focused legal blog focused on cases, policy, practical takeaways, and personal insights without the heavy “textbook” vibe.

What you’ll find here

Short, readable posts designed for students and curious readers.

  • Case notes Which summarize what happened and why it mattered.
  • Business law breakdowns (contracts, consumer law, competition, IPO's, litigation).
  • “So what?” takeaways for founders, managers, and everyday consumers.
  • Links to primary sources (courts, regulators, official docs) when possible.

Disclaimer

This blog is for general information and learning only. It is NOT legal advice, and reading it does not create a lawyer-client relationship, nor should it be mistaken for legal advice.

If you need advice for a real situation, talk to a qualified professional in your jurisdiction.

Personal objective

I wanted a place to write about real-world cases in a way that’s quick to read, easy to understand, and actually useful for business/management contexts. I strive to sharpen my legal analysis while making the law more accessible beyond the classroom.

I am a Justice and Legal Studies major with a minor in Business and Management. I take great pride in expanding my knowledge to the best of my ability and hope that what this blog stands for helps other expand theirs as well.
